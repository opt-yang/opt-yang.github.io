IMPORTANT NOTICE - DESIGN SESSION

Contents:

Design a prototype of ATM (Auto Teller Machine) INTERFACE using any related tools, including but not limited to:

- Microsoft PowerPoint/Apple Keynote/WPS/etc.;

- 3M PostIt;

- Mockups softwares;

- Drawing by hand;

- ...

There are mainly two objects of our lab session:

1. Design and implement a software prototype, e.g., ATM software for a bank. Each student should try to design a rather complete set of software functionalities.

2. Make the usability of the ATM interface AS LOW AS POSSIBLE. That is, you need to design a terrible ATM interface for users to use.

3. However, functionalities of the ATM interface should still be complete. That is, users still can withdraw and deposit their money if all the operations are correctly done.

4. Evaluation of the prototype. Each student should evaluate his/her or other student's software prototype, and surveys/writes about the reasons that the designed prototype is usable/not usable/user-friendly/not user-friendly...

The experiments can be done either on the lab computers or your own laptops/tablets/draft papers. Please send the screenshots/photos and evaluation reports of your prototype to my email address yangzhang AT whut.edu.cn before 30 MAY 2018.

IMPORTANT NOTICE - FINAL REPORTS

Every student enrolled in this class is required to submit a final report on HCI related topics

Possible topic choices include but not limited to:

- HCI applied in different countries/ethinics/ages;

- HCI evaluation of daily products, industrial devices, and so on;

- Survey/Review on HCI state-of-art;

- Extended studies of the research papers you read, or the lecture video you watched;

- ... Any other HCI related topics that you like.

There is no requirement on the length of your reports submitted. But your final scores will be based on the originality and quality of the reports. So please be considerate.

SUBMISSION POLICY: All the reports must be sent directly to my email address yangzhang AT whut.edu.cn before 15 JUNE 2018. This is a firm deadline WITHOUT any extension. After that day, final scores will be recorded in the online systems.
