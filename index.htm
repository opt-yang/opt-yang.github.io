Biography

Dr. Yang ZHANG (M'2011-) is an associate professor (2021-) with the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China. He received B. Eng., B. Eng. (Minor), and M. Eng. from Beihang University (BUAA) in 2008, 2010 and 2011, respectively. He obtained a Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2015 (Thesis), supervised by Dr. Dusit Niyato and Dr. Ping Wang. He was a visiting scholar (2014-2015) in Dr. Dong In Kim's research group at Sungkyunkwan University, Korea, and a research fellow (2019-2020) with the Energy Research Institute at NTU (ERI@N). He worked as a lecturer (assistant professor, 2015-2016) and an associate professor (2016-2021) at Wuhan University of Technology, Wuhan, China. His current research interests include: market-oriented modeling for network resource allocation, optimal decision making problems in wireless and distributed systems, multiple objective optimization, and deep reinforcement learning.

章阳，博士，南京航空航天大学计算机科学与技术学院副教授。2008年、2010、2011年分别于北京航空航天大学取得工学学士、理学学士（双学位项目）、工学硕士学位；2015年于新加坡南洋理工大学（NTU）取得博士学位（学位论文），导师为Dr. Dusit Niyato与Dr. Ping Wang。曾任武汉理工大学计算机科学与技术学院讲师（2015-2016）、副教授（2016-2021）。
